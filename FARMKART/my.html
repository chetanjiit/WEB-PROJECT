OURFEATURES

Sell with best price

Kisan market is online agriculture market place, where farmer can sell his products with competitive prices. This is beneficiary to farmers without any mediators in between. This marketplace helps agricultural and rural-based businesses to expand market share through an on-line trading community.

But directly from kisan

Buyer can directly purchase products from farmers in kisan market, it benefits both buyer and famers to facilitate convenient market to buy and sale all agriculture related products. Kisan Market does not act as a trader for an agent, nor do we have a vested interest in promoting specific deals.

Market value

Users can get detailed information on the market value of products at national and state level. Information on current bazar rates, commodity rates, weekly markets, average price trend, monthly markets, average commodity arrival trend, weekly price variations, etc. is provided.
